Biography

Currently, I am working as Reserach Associate with Prof. Nihar R. Mohapatra at Indian Institute of Technology Gandhingar. I have completed B.Tech at National Institiute of Technology(NIT) Patna majoring in Electrical Engineering . My interests include Compact Modelling of Multigate Transistors, Semiconductor Devices and Instrumentation.

At NIT Patna, I worked with Prof. Arunangshu Ghosh on Development of an interface for electronic tongue. I have completed an internship at the Nanoelectronics Devices and Circuits Lab in IIT Gandhinagar where I worked on investigation and modelling of charge centroid in III-V semiconductor based double-gate FETs for my undergraduate thesis. Additionally, I have also completed an internship at the Nanolab in IIT kanpur where I worked on modelling and parameter extraction of long-channel MOSFET using BSIM3 model during the summer of 2017. Furthermore, I have also worked on raspberry pi based automation of diagonistic system during my intership at Elangovan's lab in IIT Delhi during the summer of 2016.
